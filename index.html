How It Began

It started simply — the way the best things always do. Jason and Hossana found in each other something rare: a person who felt like home. What began as friendship quietly deepened into something neither of them could explain, only feel.

♥

Best Friends First

Before anything else, they were each other's person. Jason became Hossana's greatest blessing — her rock, her safe place, the one who showed up without being asked. And Hossana became Jason's light — the one who made ordinary days feel like something worth remembering.

♥

A Love That Grows
