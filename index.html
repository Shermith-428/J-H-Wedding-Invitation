Our Story

Two Hearts, One Church, One Beautiful Accident

Some people spend their whole lives looking for the kind of love that feels like coming home. Jason and Hosanna found it on a Sunday morning at church.

♥

Where It All Began

It started the way most beautiful things often do.
Not with grand gestures or perfectly planned moments,
but with something quiet and unhurried.
Two people, the same church, and a God who clearly had a plan neither of them saw coming. There was something about Hosanna that Jason couldn't quite shake. And something about Jason that made Hosanna smile a little longer than she meant to.

♥

Two People Who Just Fit

Jason's gentleness is the kind that wraps around you quietly, making Hosanna feel safe, loved, and completely at home in a way she never has with anyone else. He is her constant, her steady place to land, and above all else, her favourite person. And yet, with him, joy is never far away. He makes her laugh unlike anyone ever has, turning the most ordinary moments into something worth remembering. They are, at their core, each other's best friends. For Jason, Hosanna is nothing short of life-changing. She has drawn him closer to his calling, made him want to push himself, and shown him what it looks like to be truly known and loved. These two don't just love each other. They bring out the best in each other, every single day.

♥

The Proposal

Jason had been quietly planning for nearly a year, sourcing the ring long before Hosanna had any idea what was coming. With the help of friends and family who deserve an award for keeping a secret this big, he pulled it off completely.
She walked in with no idea what she was walking into. And when it hit her all at once, the excitement took over entirely. So much so that she missed Jason pouring his heart out. After an awkward pause, she asked him to pop the question.
He stared at her and said, "I DID!! Will you marry me?!"
She said YES!,
And then they both burst out laughing, because of course that is exactly how their story would go. Even in the most important moment of their lives, they turned it into something only the two of them could pull off.

♥

Forever Starts Here

Looking back, it is almost hard to believe how far they have come. And yet, neither of them can imagine a version of their story that ends any other way. From the very beginning, this was always end game. Every season they walked through, every moment they chose each other through the beautiful and the hard, was quietly leading here. On the 8th of August 2026, surrounded by everyone who loves them, Jason and Hosanna will stand before God and make it official.

Their story is a reminder that love is not just a feeling. It is a choice, made over and over again. And when you put God at the centre of everything, and keep showing up for each other, something truly beautiful is built. He is the reason they have come this far, and the foundation of everything still to come.

Here's to the beginning of forever. 🤍
