Our Story

Two Hearts, One Church, One Beautiful Accident

Some people spend their whole lives looking for the kind of love that feels like coming home. Jason and Hosanna found it on a Sunday morning at church.

♥

Where It All Began

It started the way the most beautiful things often do — not with grand gestures or perfectly planned moments, but with something quiet and unhurried. Two people, the same church, and a God who clearly had a plan neither of them saw coming. There was something about Hosanna that Jason couldn't quite shake. And something about Jason that made Hosanna smile a little longer than she meant to.

♥

Best Friends Who Fell

Before the love letters and the late-night laughter, they were simply each other's favourite person to be around. Jason, steady and warm, the kind of man who shows up, became Hosanna's safe place without even trying. And Hosanna, full of light and life, made Jason feel like every ordinary day was somehow worth celebrating. Friendship turned to something deeper the way seasons change: gradually, and then all at once.

♥

The Proposal

If you know Hosanna, you know that very little gets past her. So when Jason decided he was going to propose, he understood the assignment. Quietly planning for nearly a year, sourcing the ring long before Hosanna had any idea — which, if you know her, is nothing short of a miracle. With the help of their friends and family, who deserve their own award for keeping a secret this big, Jason pulled off the impossible. She walked in with no idea what she was walking into. And then it hit her all at once.

Forever Starts Here

Four years of choosing each other through the beautiful and the ordinary, the hilarious and the hard. Four years of building something neither of them could have dreamed up alone. On the 8th of August 2026, surrounded by everyone who loves them, Jason and Hosanna will make it official — though if you ask either of them, it has been decided for a long time.

Here's to the beginning of forever. 🤍
